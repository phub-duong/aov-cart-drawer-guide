# Quota Usage for Free Plan

Learn how the Free plan order limit works and what happens when you reach it.

## Order Limit

The Free plan allows up to **50 orders per month** processed through the cart drawer. This means 50 orders where the customer uses the slide cart to complete their purchase.

## What Happens When You Reach the Limit

When your store exceeds 50 cart drawer orders in a billing cycle:

- The cart drawer **stops appearing** for new customers.
- Existing cart sessions in progress are not interrupted.
- You will see a notification in the app dashboard indicating you've reached the limit.

## Quota Reset

Your order count resets at the start of each **30-day billing cycle** (counted from your installation date). After reset:

- The counter goes back to zero.
- The cart drawer becomes active again automatically.

## How to Check Your Usage

1. Open **AOV.ai Cart Drawer** in your Shopify admin.
2. Check the **Dashboard** — your current order count and remaining quota are displayed.

## Removing the Limit

Upgrade to any paid plan (Basic, Pro, Advanced, or Plus) to get **unlimited orders** through the cart drawer. Your plan is determined by your Shopify subscription tier — see [Pricing Plan](pricing-plan.md) for details.
