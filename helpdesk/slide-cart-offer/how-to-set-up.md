# How to Set Up Slide Cart Offer

## What is Slide Cart Offer?

Slide Cart Offer integrates with the **AOV.ai Free Gifts** app to display promotional campaigns directly inside the cart drawer. This includes Buy X Get Y deals, gifts with cart value, quantity-based gifts, and more — all shown in a dedicated "Today's offer" tab within the cart.

![Slide Cart Offer in cart drawer](/.gitbook/assets/slide-cart-offer/what-is.png)

## Why use Slide Cart Offer?

- **Powerful promotions in the cart** — Show free gift offers right where the buying decision happens.
- **Seamless integration** — The AOV Free Gifts app handles the campaign logic while the cart drawer handles the display.
- **Multiple campaign types** — Support for Buy X Get Y, Gift With Cart Value, Gift With Quantity Purchase, Gift With Collection Value, and Milestone Rewards.

## Requirements before using

- You must have the **AOV.ai Cart Drawer** app installed and active.
- You must install the **AOV.ai Free Gifts** app (available from the Shopify App Store).
- At least one promotion campaign must be created and active in the Free Gifts app.

## How to Set Up?

### Step 1: Install the AOV.ai Free Gifts App

1. Go to **AOV.ai Cart Drawer > Cart Editor**.
2. Click **Slide cart offer (AOV Free Gifts)** in the left menu under **Body**.
3. If the app is **Not installed**, click **Install app** to install AOV.ai Free Gifts from the Shopify App Store.
4. If the app is already **Installed**, you will see a green "Installed" badge. Click **View app** to open the Free Gifts app.

<!-- screenshot: Slide cart offer page showing install status and Install app button | page: /cart-editor | element: .slide-cart-offer-install | annotate: yes -->

![Install AOV Free Gifts app](/.gitbook/assets/slide-cart-offer/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Create a Promotion Campaign

1. Open the **AOV.ai Free Gifts** app.
2. Create a new promotion campaign. Available campaign types include:
   - **Buy X Get Y** — Buy specific items and get a free product (e.g., "Buy bed, get a free chair").
   - **Gift With Cart Value** — Spend more, get a free gift or discount (e.g., "Spend $250, get a free table").
   - **Milestone Rewards** — Spend more to unlock exciting rewards (e.g., "Spend $25 for 10% off, $75 for free gift").
   - **Gift With Quantity Purchase** — Buy a total number of items to get a discount (e.g., "Buy 3 get 20% off").
   - **Gift With Collection Value** — Buy from select collections to get a gift or discount.
3. Save the campaign.

<!-- screenshot: AOV Free Gifts app campaign creation | page: /free-gift | element: .free-gift-campaigns | annotate: no -->

![Create promotion campaign](/.gitbook/assets/slide-cart-offer/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Enable the Today's Offer Tab

1. Go back to **AOV.ai Cart Drawer > Cart Editor > Slide cart offer (AOV Free Gifts)**.
2. Under the setup instructions, go to **Step 3**: Click the **Today's offer** tab.
3. Click **Enable** to activate the slide cart offer display in the cart drawer.

<!-- screenshot: Today's offer tab with Enable button | page: /cart-editor | element: .slide-cart-offer-enable | annotate: yes -->

![Enable Today's offer tab](/.gitbook/assets/slide-cart-offer/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Preview and Save

1. Review the slide cart offer in the live preview on the right panel.
2. Click **Save** to apply your settings.
3. If you need assistance, click **contact us** for more support.

<!-- screenshot: Preview showing slide cart offer with Save button | page: /cart-editor | element: button:has-text('Save') | annotate: yes -->

![Preview and save](/.gitbook/assets/slide-cart-offer/step-4.png)

Your slide cart offer is now connected to the AOV Free Gifts app. Customers will see your promotional campaigns directly inside the cart drawer! 🚀
