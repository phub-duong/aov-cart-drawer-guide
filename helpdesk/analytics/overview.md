# Analytics Overview

## What is Analytics?

The Analytics section gives you a quick snapshot of how your cart drawer is performing. You can track total impressions (how many times the cart drawer has been viewed) and check the embed status to make sure the cart drawer is properly installed on your store.

## Why monitor Analytics?

- **Track engagement** — Total impressions tell you how often customers interact with the cart drawer.
- **Verify installation** — The embed status confirms whether the cart drawer is correctly embedded and visible on your storefront.
- **Measure impact** — Use impression data to understand traffic patterns and evaluate the effectiveness of your cart drawer features.

## Requirements before using

- You must have the **AOV.ai Cart Drawer** app installed and active.

## How to Use Analytics

### Step 1: Open the Analytics Dashboard

1. Go to **AOV.ai Cart Drawer** and look at the **Overview** section on the home page.
2. You will see two key metrics displayed.

<!-- screenshot: Overview section on the home page showing metrics | page: / | element: .overview-section | annotate: no -->

![Analytics dashboard](/.gitbook/assets/analytics/step-1.png)

### Step 2: Check Total Impressions

1. The **Total impressions** metric shows the total number of times your cart drawer has been opened by visitors.
2. Use this number to gauge customer engagement with the cart drawer over time.

<!-- screenshot: Total impressions metric card | page: / | element: .total-impressions-card | annotate: no -->

![Total impressions](/.gitbook/assets/analytics/step-2.png)

### Step 3: Verify Embed Status

1. The **Embed status** shows whether the cart drawer is properly embedded on your live store.
   - **On** — The cart drawer is active and visible to customers.
   - **Off** — The cart drawer is not embedded. Click **Embed settings** to configure.
2. If the status shows **Off**, click **Embed settings** to open the embed configuration and follow the instructions to activate the cart drawer.

<!-- screenshot: Embed status indicator showing On/Off with Embed settings link | page: / | element: .embed-status-card | annotate: yes -->

![Embed status](/.gitbook/assets/analytics/step-3.png)

### Step 4: Take Action Based on Data

1. If impressions are low, consider:
   - Enabling the **Sticky Cart** button to make the cart more accessible.
   - Toggling **Open Cart Drawer when an item is added to the cart** in General Settings.
   - Checking that the embed status is **On**.
2. If impressions are healthy, explore features like **Upsell Products**, **Rewards Bar**, and **Announcements** to maximize the value of each cart view.

<!-- screenshot: Home page with overview metrics and quick action buttons | page: / | element: .home-overview | annotate: no -->

![Take action based on data](/.gitbook/assets/analytics/step-4.png)

Keep an eye on your analytics to ensure the cart drawer is working at its best. Data-driven decisions lead to higher conversions! 🚀
