# Welcome

**AOV.ai Cart Drawer** replaces your store's default cart page with a sleek, slide-out drawer that keeps shoppers on the page while they browse and add items. It comes packed with conversion-boosting features like upsells, rewards bars, discount codes, and trust badges — all designed to increase your average order value.

## Who is this for?

This guide is for Shopify merchants using the **AOV.ai Cart Drawer** app. Whether you are setting up the app for the first time or fine-tuning individual features, you will find step-by-step instructions for every setting.

## Getting started

1. Install the [AOV.ai Cart Drawer](https://apps.shopify.com/cart-drawer) app from the Shopify App Store.
2. Open the app and follow the **Setup guide** to enable the cart drawer and match your brand.
3. Use the **Cart editor** to customize every section of the drawer.
4. Explore individual features like **Upsell Products**, **Rewards Bar**, and **Announcements** to maximize conversions.

If you need help at any time, click the **Help Center** icon inside the app to chat with our support team.
